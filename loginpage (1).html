India has come a long way since its independence and is today the world’s third largest economy. While agriculture is the mainstay of our GDP, IT and ITES have a fair share in shaping our country’s growth. Over the past couple of decades many of our cities have seen unprecedented growth in infrastructure and manpower. However, this has also resulted in more cars hitting our roads, creating regular traffic snarls. Wings Travels was established in 1994 with vision of making travel easy. To bring order to the chaos that we face on our city roads, Wings Travels introduced the concept of car pooling in the year 2008 and offers this service across cities of Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Goa, & Pune.
